About

The University strives to form a new generation of IT specialists who are ready to work in conditions of rapid technological progress and global digitalization. UPIT is aimed at combining theoretical training with practical skills that are in demand in real work.

Key areas of study and research:

  1. Artificial intelligence and machine learning – the development of intelligent systems capable of analyzing data, predicting events and automating processes.
  2. Cybersecurity – ensuring the security of digital systems and data, including protection against hacker attacks and the development of encryption tools.
  3. Software development is the creation of applications, web services and other IT products for business, science and the social sphere.
  4. Internet of Things (IoT) technologies – development of solutions for combining physical devices into a single intelligent network.
  5. Multimedia technology and design is a combination of technology and creativity to create digital content.
